Residency in Bloom

How to Join

Becoming a resident of Bloom is straightforward. Residency may be granted by the Speaker, the President, any Congress member, or a Secretary. The quickest way to get started is to join our Discord and introduce yourself.

01

Join the Bloom Discord server and introduce yourself in the appropriate channel.

02

A government member will grant you the Resident role in-game, giving you access to property ownership and factory access within Bloom.

03

After one week as a Resident with Bloom as your primary nation, you become eligible to apply for full Citizenship.

Citizenship Tiers
Tier I
Resident

The entry level of membership in Bloom. Residents have the right to own property and access factories within Bloom's borders. Requirements Granted by the Speaker, President, a Congress member, or a Secretary. Players with a history of treason, exile, or affiliation with nations at war with Bloom are ineligible.

Tier II
Citizen

Full citizens are eligible to vote in elections, hold elected and non-elected offices, and access non-botable public farms. Requirements Must hold Resident status for a minimum of one week, and must maintain Bloom as their primary nation. Elevated by unanimous agreement of the Speaker and President.

Tier III
Dignitary

The highest citizenship tier. Dignitaries receive access to botable farms and elite smelter rates at the public exchange. Requirements Must hold Citizen status for one month and complete qualifying jobs. Apply via an official Discord ticket. Approved unanimously by the Speaker and President.
