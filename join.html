Citizenship

How to Join Bloom

Residency may be granted by the Speaker, the President, any Congress member, or a Secretary. Join our Discord and introduce yourself to get started.

01  —  Join the Bloom Discord and introduce yourself.

02  —  A government member grants you the Resident role in-game.

03  —  After one week as a Resident, you may apply for full Citizenship.

Citizenship Tiers

Resident

Entry level membership. Grants property ownership and factory access within Bloom. Eligible players are granted residency by any government member.

Citizen

Full voting rights, eligible to hold office, and access to non-botable public farms. Requires one week as a Resident with Bloom as your primary nation.

Dignitary

The highest tier. Access to botable farms and elite smelter rates. Requires one month as a Citizen and completion of qualifying jobs.
