How to Join Bloom

Residency can be granted by the Speaker, the President, any Congress member, or a Secretary. The easiest way to start is through Discord.

01

Join the Bloom Discord and introduce yourself.

02

A government member grants you the Resident role in-game.

03

After one week, you may apply for full Citizenship.
