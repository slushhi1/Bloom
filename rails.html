A small number of international connections are also accessible via the overland station's southern branch. All international connections are coordinated by the Secretary of Transportation and require Speaker approval.

Employment

Work for the Bloom Rail Company

Bloom Rail Company

The Bloom Rail Company (BRC) is Bloom's state rail operator, responsible for the construction, maintenance, and expansion of all rail infrastructure. Citizens and residents can take on rail jobs through the standard Bloom jobs system. Rail jobs range from tunnel digging and track laying to station builds and ice road installation. All BRC jobs are paid and may count toward Dignitary qualification.

To apply for a rail job, open a ticket in the Bloom Discord and specify which role you are interested in.

Tunnel Digger Excavation of new tunnel routes
Track Layer Rail and ice road installation
Station Builder Construction of station buildings
Maintenance Worker Upkeep of existing lines
