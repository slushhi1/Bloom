Employment

Work for BRC

The Bloom Rail Company operates all rail infrastructure in the Republic. Citizens and residents can take on paid jobs through the Bloom jobs system. All BRC work counts toward Dignitary qualification. Open a Discord ticket to apply.

Tunnel Digger

Excavation of new tunnel routes meeting BRC specifications for width, height, and lighting.

Track Layer

Rail and packed ice installation once tunnels are complete, including hybrid ice road sections.

Station Builder

Design and construction of station buildings following Bloom's Naboo-inspired build style.

Maintenance Worker

Upkeep and repair of existing lines to keep all active routes operational.
