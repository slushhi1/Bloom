About Us

The Republic of Bloom is a longstanding buildfriend democracy on CivMC. Our capital city is themed after Naboo from the Star Wars prequel trilogy. Government is handled by an elected Congress of five to seven members, with two-month terms and in-game elections conducted via signed books at the Capitol Building. Bloom has an active job market and ongoing construction projects for citizens looking to earn income.
