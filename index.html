Government

The Republic of Bloom is governed by an elected Congress of seven members, chosen by citizens every two months through in-game elections conducted via signed books at the Capitol Building. Congress elects a Speaker who serves as Head of State alongside the President — a permanent executive role currently held by Slushhi.

Legislature
The Congress

Seven elected members serving two-month terms. Citizens cast votes in person at the Capitol using signed books. Congress holds legislative authority, creates law by simple majority, and may impeach officials by two-thirds vote.

Head of State
The Speaker

Elected by Congress from among its members by simple majority. The Speaker presides over trials, may enact Official Acts for temporary matters, and approves or denies proposals after voting concludes. Term length mirrors Congress — two months.

Executive
The President

A permanent role currently held by Slushhi. The President holds ownership of all Bloom namelayer groups, may veto constitutional changes, and may block impeachments. Eligible to serve in Congress and other offices like any citizen.
