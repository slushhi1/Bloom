Governance

The Government of Bloom

The Republic of Bloom is governed by an elected Congress of seven members chosen by citizens every two months. Congress elects a Speaker who serves as Head of State. A permanent President holds executive authority over Bloom's namelayer groups and constitutional matters. Together, these three branches uphold the rule of law and the will of Bloomeans.

Three Branches
Legislature
The Congress

Seven elected members serving two-month terms. Citizens vote in person at the Capitol Building using signed books. Congress passes laws by simple majority, may propose constitutional amendments by two-thirds majority, and holds impeachment power over any official including the Speaker. A vacant seat triggers a special election within three days.

Head of State
The Speaker

Elected by Congress from among its members by simple majority. The Speaker presides over all trials, issues Official Acts for temporary matters, and has final approval or denial power over all proposals after voting concludes. The Speaker may be removed by a two-thirds congressional vote with presidential approval. Term mirrors Congress — two months. The current Speaker is Xcios.

Executive
The President

A permanent role currently held by Slushhi. The President holds ownership of all Bloom namelayer groups, may veto constitutional amendments, and may block impeachments. The President is eligible to serve in Congress and hold additional offices like any citizen, but holds limited day-to-day governing power.

Cabinet Secretaries
—
Secretary of Transportation

Appointed by Congress. Manages Bloom's rail station and oversees construction, maintenance, and expansion of rail networks. Coordinates connections with other nations.

—
Secretary of Agriculture

Appointed by Congress. Manages all state-owned farms, oversees XP production, and coordinates fair distribution of resources among citizens.

—
Secretary of Interior

Appointed by Congress. Approves new developments, oversees public works, decrees zoning regulations, and manages derelictions within Bloom.

—
Secretary of State

Appointed by the Speaker. Represents Bloom in all foreign relations and serves as the primary diplomat to foreign nations and entities.

—
Secretary of Defense

Appointed by the President. Manages the Bloom Vault, defensive namelayer groups, bunkers, and all critical defense infrastructure including iceroads.

—
Secretary of Treasury

Appointed by Congress. Serves as custodian of the Monument Bank account, tracks all transactions, and maintains transparent financial records.

—
Secretary of Archives

Appointed by the President. Maintains the full catalog of laws, amendments, government documents, and election materials. Oversees voter registration and residency verification.

—
Secretary of Education

Appointed by Congress. Oversees Bloom University in all capacities, manages the admission and education of new residents, and appoints Initiates' Guides.
