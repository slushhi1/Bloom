Citizenship Tiers
Resident

For new players and those based in another nation. Select farms, factories, and Bloom channels. Can build in Lilly.

Citizen

Bloom as your primary nation. All farms, eligible to vote and run in elections. Bloom City builds require approval.

Dignitary

Awarded for significant contributions. Elite smelter rates at the ore exchange.

Building Areas
Bloom City

Theed Naboo style. Approval required — selective but open to all.

Lilly

Suburb. Build in Bloom style without formal approval.
